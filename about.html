About Rainydays

Rainydays creates outerwear for the active hiker or the active city person. Either way, our jackets are made of sustainable material that is made to last. Our vision is for you to be comfortable in good quality and stay dry during your adventures.

History behind our brand

After many years of planning and dreaming Rainydays finally saw daylight and became a reality in August 2020. Rainydays stands for exploring, sustainability, activeness and comfortness.
